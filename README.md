“Smart Pet Care” is a website designed to connect dog owners with trusted caretakers. Pet parents can create detailed profiles for their dogs, including photos, age, medicines, weight, breed, bio descriptions, local zip code where they reside, and important details such as whether their dog is hypoallergenic. Caretakers can then browse these profiles and connect directly with owners through a secure messaging system(within the website) to arrange walks, pet sitting, expected pay, and other care services. Caretakers will also have profiles featuring bios and reviews, allowing pet parents to easily evaluate their experience and reliability before choosing the right caretaker for their pet. Whether you’re a dog owner looking for reliable support or someone hoping to earn extra income by caring for others pets, “Smart Pet Care” makes the process simple, safe, and rewarding.
